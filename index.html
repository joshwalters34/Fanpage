Bio

Taylor Alison Swift was born in Reading, PA on December 13, 1989. Taylor was named after singer-songwriter James Taylor. In 2004 she moved to Nasheville, TN to pursue a career in the music industry. Among her many awards, Taylor has won 10 Grammy Awards, an Emmy Award, 32 American Music Awards, and was inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame. In addition to her music career, she's appeared in movies like "Valentine's Day" and "Cats."
